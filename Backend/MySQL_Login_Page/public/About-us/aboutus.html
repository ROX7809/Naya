About Us
☰

Ride Sathi is a ride-sharing platform for the Kathmandu University community, offering efficient, cost-effective, and eco-friendly transportation for students, faculty, and staff.

Why RideSathi?

RideSathi offers Kathmandu University members a cost-effective and time-efficient way to commute. By sharing rides, you save on travel expenses and easy daily commute, all while building connections within the university community.

What is RideSathi’s mission?

To offer efficient, affordable, and eco-friendly tran sportation for students, faculty, and staff, fostering community and supporting sustainable travel to enhance the campus experience.

What RideSathi Does?

Provides an easy-to-use platform for Kathmandu University members to share rides, cutting travel costs and boosting commuting efficiency. Our service saves time and money while enhancing engagement and connectivity, fostering a vibrant and supportive campus community.
